Enhanced Modular Generation

General Info

In game development we use systems such as geometry nodes to generate procedural 3D assets.

Enhanced Modular Generation is a technique and a specification for it.

In short, it allows us to decouple a procedural system into modular parts improving:

- Versatility

- Simplicity

- Reusability

- Scalability

Simplicity

Traditional procedural generation systems grow in complexity as the feature set increases.

Complexity causes development friction and slows progress.

Modularizing the features into self-contained systems can simplify the process and stop complexity from growing out of hand.

More Intelligent Procedural Systems

Enhanced Modular Generation means systems can communicate with a data interface.

This enables us to compose large intelligent networks of procedural objects.

Best described as emergence, the whole composition of all procedural objects is greater than the sum of its parts.
