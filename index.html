Landscape Grass System

Landscape material that uses vertex color to blend between layers so environment artists can paint paths. Grass and small foliage is automatically instanced on the grass layer. All textures are projected top-down.

Layers are height blended

Path height is also used for color selection

Grass mesh has vertical split normals to inherit ground shading

Grass material increases hue & saturation as it rises from the ground
