Drift-based Ai Pathing

The AI needed a custom pathing solution to handle the physics.

Instead of just moving in a direction, the AI indirectly control the direction they move in by controlling:

- Throttle

- Brakes

- Turning

The hardest part was writing the algorithm to allow AI to know where they are headed.

Snakelike Dungeon Generator

On game start, a procedural level is made.

Essentially, play a random game of snake, then after the snake reaches a certain size, turn it's body into rooms for a rougelike dungeon.

Algorithm

Start at origin, and place a room

1. Generate a random direction

2. Open the door in that direction

3. Move in that direction

4. Place a room

5. Open the door from the direction you just moved

6. Repeat
