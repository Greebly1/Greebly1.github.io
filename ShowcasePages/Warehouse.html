Warehouse

General Info

First-person horror game set in a warehouse at night made with Unreal Engine 5.

The player is tasked with locating crates and bringing them to depot locations.

The warehouse uses motion-activated lights during nightshift.

If the player hears a heartbeat in the darkness, turning the light on will end in a loss.

If the player lingers in the darkness for too long, they lose.

Environment Art

All visual assets of this game were made from scratch.

Materials use PBR shading to tie it into the lighting.

Textures are low-resolution, sampled using 'nearest' filtering method to preserve pixel sharpness.

Combined multiple light types to compose aesthetic lighting.

Lastly, a starry night skybox can be seen through windows on the ceiling.
